Our History and Ambitions

Building a vibrant German-speaking academic and cultural community at the University of Geneva.

The German-Speaking Students’ Association of the University of Geneva was founded on 19 October 2025. It was officially registered as an affiliated association of the University on 25 November 2025.

While our history is still short, our ambitions are high.

The goal of the association is to promote the learning and use of the German language.

Through group activities, we aim to create a sense of home for German-speaking students and a welcoming learning environment for all Germanophiles.
